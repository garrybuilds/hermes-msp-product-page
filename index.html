How It Works With Your Stack

We don't replace your tools. We connect to them and automate the work between them.

Your field techs keep using ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, or Jobber on their phones. Your office team keeps using QuickBooks. Your sales team keeps using your CRM. Hermes agents sit on top of these tools, automating the work between them — the follow-ups, the data entry, the scheduling coordination, the reporting.

We're the automation layer, not the replacement layer.

Level A — Direct API

Real-time or near-real-time connection. Agent reads and writes via REST or GraphQL. Examples: ServiceTitan, HubSpot, QuickBooks, Google Calendar.

Latency: minutes · Reliability: 99.5%

Level B — Webhook

Event-driven. Agent receives webhooks from the tool and responds immediately. Examples: Stripe, Cal.com, form submissions.

Latency: seconds · Reliability: 99%

Level C — Export / Email

Agent works with exported data (CSV, PDF, email parsing) or scheduled sync. For legacy tools with no API. Slower, less reliable.

Latency: hours · Reliability: 95%

Data Accuracy Guarantee

We can't guarantee a field tech enters perfect data. What we can guarantee:

  • We catch bad data before it propagates downstream
  • We flag it immediately in your portal with a ticket
  • We route it to the right person to fix

The agent is a data quality filter, not just a data mover.
